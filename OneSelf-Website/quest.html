The 1self quest

Smarter living for all of us

Unprecedented data is being collected about everybody’s interactions with the world. From data, we can create insights and knowledge. Through insights and knowledge we can create change. We can use all of this data for our own personal benefit. What’s more, we can and should use this data for the good of humanity.

Imagine if the connected and aggregated (but not personally identifiable) data about the activities of people became a resource for everyone, like a Wikipedia of human data. Imagine if smart brains from all over the world could interrogate this data and find out interesting and useful insights for the benefit of everybody. Perhaps there’s a cure for cancer lurking in the everyday activities of the population. Certainly there’s information that would help us be healthier, more energy efficient and more environmentally friendly.

In a world where data is becoming pervasive, the 1self quest is to unleash the value of this data to you and to our collective humanity. The 1self quest is to help humanity get smarter.
