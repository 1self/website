Your phone, apps, devices, websites you visit and all the things you interact with in the world are collecting data about you. 1self gives you the power to unite your data for your own benefit.

Understand yourself

How well do you really know yourself?

How many of hours did you work last week? How much sleep did you get? What did you eat? What did you spend money on?

1self can give you the answers to these question and more. By collecting your data in one place you'll understand yourself and what really makes you tick.

Tell stories

Where did you travel? How far did you run? How many gigs have you been to? How long is your commute? Why don't you sleep well on Sundays?

With 1self, you can tell stories about your life using the data that describes your activities. Share your data visualisations to start discussion and debate.

Compare with friends or the rest of the world

Do you drink more than other people your age? Where are you in your city's commute time league table? Do you travel more than your friends?

You can be curious or you can be competitive. Either way, comparisons give you new ways to consider your activities.

Get insights

Data doesn't tell you much on its own.

1self brings your data to life by automatically connecting and correlating it to produce interesting, useful and actionable insights about your life.

Maybe, if you go to bed earlier you could earn more money. Or if you took a different route to work you'd be less stressed all day. With 1Self you can find out.
