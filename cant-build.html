Unleash the data!

Unleashing all of our personal data to help us all live smarter lives is a momentous quest and immense task. But the impact on each of our lives will be awesome.

All of the data that you create as you go about your life, collected in your own record for your own benefit without having to worry about being exploited for it.

Any data. Every niche

You lead a busy life but whatever you're up to 1self will be there helping you live it smarter.

Apps, websites and devices capture the details about your life and 1self can integrate with them so that your activity data is at your disposal.

By using 1self you'll be able to choose which data is important or interesting to you.

Integration integrity

The apps, websites and devices that you use collect different data in different ways. It'd be impossible for us to create integrations for them all.

However, large tasks become small when divided amongst many people and that's why 1self lets developers easily build integrations for the data that's interesting to them and to easily integrate their apps, websites and devices.

In return 1self can help apps, websites and devices be more popular and engaging.
