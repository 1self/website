Integration Requests

Below are some of the integrations that our users have already requested for 1self.

Remember, once the data is in 1self, the user will be able to make lots of use of it. They'll be able to see visualizations of the data by different time periods (by day, by week, by month). They'll be able to compare their activities with friends. They'll be able to see trends of change over time. They'll be able to correlate one piece of data with another (do I take more photos on a sunny day?) They'll get prompted with interesting insights ("yesterday you listened to the most minutes of music ever!").

So, when you're considering which integration to build, think about all of the interesting outputs that could come from that data :)

  • Email
    • Number of emails sent/received and who to/from.
  • Activity trackers
    • Fitbit, Misfit Shine, Nike Fuelband, Jawbone, Moves.
    • Our users would love a way to unify the data that they collect with these different devices. Once you've done one it'll probably be easy to
  • Withings Scales (and other Withings products)
    • Withings provide a good API. There are lots of interesting analyses that could be done from the data collected by the Withings scales. Also, they're a very cool bit of kit - if you like data then you'll love them!
  • eCommerce (particularly Amazon and supermarkets)
    • Allow users to see how much they're spending and on what. Later the information could be expanded to show how much fruit and veg people buy per week, for example.
    • This is a bit of a tricker one as eCommerce companies don't tend to open up APIs to give access to what people bought. However, all the information tends to be available in the order confirmation emails sent by these companies, or within the DOM when the order confirmation pages are shown...
  • Social media interactions
    • Twitter, Instagram, Foursquare, Facebook, Pinterest...
    • Who did they follow? How many pictures did they take? Where did they check in? Who liked their post? Loads of interesting activity data here
  • Vim, Emacs
    • Plug-ins to these or other developer tools that log how much time spent actively coding
  • Git log
    • Parse the git log to save the time of commits and commit comments
  • Travel cards (e.g. Oyster)
    • Where and when did you travel? Which stations/stops did you go to?
  • Weather
    • What was the weather? Temperature? Wind speed? Pollen count? Keep a user's personal weather record
  • Browser extensions
    • Browser extensions have access to the DOM. What interesting data could be picked up?
  • Various Quantified Self devices
    • Loads of Quantified Self gadgets all waiting to be integrated with 1self
