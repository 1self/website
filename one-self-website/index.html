Websites

From Amazon and ASOS to Zappos and Zillow, every website that you visit collects information about you. What you bought, how long you spent looking. Those companies use your data to personalise your experience and sell to you more effectively. With 1self, you can collect that information and use it yourself.

Apps

Your phone is the hub around which your world of data spins. It’s laden with sensors and apps, all of which are constantly collecting data about you. Currently that data is hived off and kept separate within each of your apps and you don’t even get to see most of that information. With 1self, you can bring your phone data to life by collating it and connecting it and deriving new insights from it.

Things

Have you heard of The Internet of Things? If not, you soon will. There are already 25 billion internet connected “things” in the world and that’s growing fast. These things range from buses to sneakers and from bracelets to surfboards. These things collect data about the environment they’re in and that environment often includes you. What are they saying about you? With 1self, you can collect that data and find out for yourself.

Tell stories

How far did you run? Where did you travel? How many gigs have you been to?

With 1self, you can tell stories about your life using the data that describes your activities. By sharing your data you will start discussion and debate.

Get insights

Data doesn’t tell you much on its own. 1self brings your data to life by automatically connecting and correlating it to produce interesting, useful and actionable insights about your life.

Maybe, if you go to bed earlier you could earn more money. Or if you took a different route to work you’d be less stressed all day. With 1Self you can find out.

Understand yourself

How well do you really know yourself? How many hours did you work last week? How much sleep did you get? What did you eat? What did you spend money on?

1self can give you the actual answers these questions and more. By collecting your data in one place you’ll understand yourself and what really makes you tick.

Compare

With 1self, you can compare yourself to your friends, people in your team, people who live near you or the whole rest of the world.

You can be curious or you can be competitive. Either way, comparisons give you new ways to consider your activities.
