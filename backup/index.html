Mission

1self is an open, transparent and not for profit organisation with a mission to improve the lives of individuals and of humanity overall through personal and collective insight.

Collect

Your phone, travel card, credit cards, all the apps you use, all the websites you visit and all the things you interact with in the world are collecting data about you. 1self gives you the power to use that data for your own benefit.

Personal

1self puts you in the middle of your world of personal data; a world that will provide you with entertainment, personal stories you can share, a new level of self-awareness and insights that could help you become healthier, wealthier, wiser and happier.

Insights

1self is nurturing a community who will dissect the mountains of personal data to find the information that is important to you. What’s more, the 1self community will have the richest ever open human dataset, which will enable new knowledge and insights about humanity as a whole.
